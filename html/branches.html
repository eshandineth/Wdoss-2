Lavender Beach Retreat


Lavender Beach Retreat in Galle is a coastal haven where the soothing scent of lavender merges with the gentle ocean waves. Overlooking the turquoise expanse, the hotel offers a tranquil escape.
The well-appointed rooms and suites provide modern comfort with a touch of coastal elegance. Guests wake up to ocean views and relax on private balconies. The retreat offers personalized hospitality, from savoring local dishes at the restaurant to rejuvenating spa experiences. An infinity pool overlooks the ocean, while Galle Fort and water sports await nearby.
Lavender Beach Retreat, Galle, is a sanctuary where lavender-infused serenity meets the allure of the beach, perfect for a romantic escape or a peaceful family vacation.

Lavender Safari Lodge


Lavender Safari Lodge, Yala, is an enchanting escape amidst the untamed beauty of Sri Lanka's wilderness. Nestled in the heart of Yala, this lodge offers a unique blend of tranquility and adventure.
The lodge's rustic charm harmonizes with the rugged landscape, providing an authentic safari experience. Accommodations are thoughtfully designed, allowing guests to immerse themselves in nature while enjoying modern comforts.
From Lavender Safari Lodge, embark on exhilarating wildlife safaris through Yala National Park, home to diverse flora and fauna, including the elusive leopard.
Relishing in delectable local cuisine and sharing tales of the day's explorations. The lodge's commitment to sustainable practices enhances the connection with nature.

Lavender Mountain Escape


Lavender Mountain Escape, Nuwara Eliya, is a charming retreat nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka's lush hill country. Surrounded by misty mountains and vibrant green landscapes, this sanctuary offers a serene getaway from the bustling world.
The hotel's cozy accommodations are designed to provide comfort and tranquility, allowing guests to unwind and recharge. From Lavender Mountain Escape, guests can explore the enchanting beauty of Nuwara Eliya, known for its tea plantations,
waterfalls, and cool climate. Whether indulging in nature walks, tea tasting, or simply enjoying the breathtaking mountain views, this retreat is a haven where the allure of lavender meets the serenity of the mountains, promising an unforgettable escape.
